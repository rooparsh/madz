ABOUT US

EXPLOGRAMMERS was an initiative taken by the seniors of 2012 batch with a main objective to develop a technical atmosphere in the department.

OBJECTIVES

The group started with 2, then increasing day by day to become a group of 60 with students of every year.It is basically the programming group to enhance the coding skills in a technical atmosphere of the group.

DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMME

We have a special programme called developer's programme, to make our juniors better programmers than us. We provide solutions to any query, any doubt and even teach programming to enthusiasts.

WORKS

Organized a programming tech-fest "CODE SCRATCHER 2013". It was the biggest success in the history of UCoE, with first day registrations counting 150+.This tech-fest was orgazined without the aid of any faculty.

Organized a programming competition "COD-FUN-FIESTA 2014".

Organized a workshop, where experts from the group itself, taught about their fields of expertise, namely iOS app development and python game development.

Have organized tech talks with experts from computer industry from time to time.

Launched a technical magazine 'E-Tech' for Android, iOS and Windows platform.

Have worked with many satisfied clients.
