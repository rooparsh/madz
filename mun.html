ABOUT US

Model United Nations , also known as Model UN or MUN is an educational simulation and academic competition in which students learn about diplomacy, international relations and the United Nations. MUN involves and teaches research, public speaking ,debating and writing skills, in addition to critical thinking, teamwork, and leadership abilities.Participants in Model UN conferences, referred to a delegates, are placed in commities and assigned countries, or occasionally other organizations or political figures, to represent.


Gain insight into the workings of the United Nations and the dynamics of the international relations by providing oppurtunities to assume the roles of UN representatives.

Understand relations between countries and particularly power relations between rich and poor countries.

Carry out in-depth research which will include geography, history, science and economics related to climate change and issues of global poverty and the MDGs.







OBJECTIVES


Debate the issues that confront world leaders adn draft resolutions in response to these global issues.

Develop and experience important socail skills such as teamwork and group cooperation,conflict resolution, negotiation, consensus-buildin , critical thinking, publiv speaking and listening.
