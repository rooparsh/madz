ABOUT US

PUNJABI BEAT BREAKERZ is a bhangra group based in UCoE(Punjabi University, patiala).

CLUBS

The basic idea of forming the group was to restablish the dying passion for bhangra especially among engineering colleges. The main objective of the group is to keep experimenting with their steps and moves; Revolutionizing the folk dance by keepin its actual soul intact.

The group has been victorious at different stages like NIT Kurukshetra , PEC etc. The group is still working for popularizing the folk dance and keeping Punjabi's love for this art alive.
