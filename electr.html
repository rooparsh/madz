ABOUT US

MADS is the collaboration of all the student clubs working actively in their respective fields

OBJECTIVES

To coordinate between all the student clubs.To enhance their performances by their collaboration


To provide better services to students. To teach them professional skills just in their college, to raise their level and connecting them with other students.

CLUBS

The student clubs are considered to be the networking life lines of the student life in colleges and universities. The clubs give you the chance to apply classroom learning to the outside world.This helps you to gain invaluable leadership as well as life skills.

In addtition to connecting you with other students, who have similar interests, professional clubs are a platform to interact with alumni and professionals in their field of interest.

TEACHING
PROGRAMME

MADS is launching a teaching programme in which students can learn professional and life skills in their college only from the students who excel in those skills for free or at very reasonable charge.

They can learn these skills by sitting in their own colleges by not compromising with their class- timings.
