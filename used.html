ABOUT US

USEDLESS INDIAN

The USEDLESS INDIAN refers to us- The Indians. It is an attitude that suggests that no one is useless. It is just that we're used less

This organisation is aimed upon giving a new direction to the youth of our nation.Originally, based in Punjabi University, Patiala, we work upon bringing the inspiring youth to the forefront.

We aim upon developing a clean INDIA for clean minds and clean streets top the need of the hour. Our organization showcase awareness and is meant to inspire the youth. So that they would be able to make the most if their UNUSED capabilities.


People:Break the conventions, folow your heart because the change 'U' want to see in this world should begin with 'U'.
