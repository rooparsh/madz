ABOUT US

The camera club is dedicated to stimulating interest in photography and improving the photographic skills of its members and to promote the art of photography

The aim of UCoE Camera Club, simply put, is to provide a venue where like-minded people ,interested in the art of photogrraphy, can come together and exchange information, learn more about their interest, improve their techniques and share images in various formats in a congenial atmosphere. This enables events to be scheduled for members to try their practical skills in table top and portraiture events.










The club's meeting place provides a space where the objectives may be met. For practical evenings it is possible to arrange upto half a dozen sets, each being overseen by a knowledgeable member who can give advice and help with diffculties. Similarly there is a space set aside for inexperienced members to partake in tutorials on various subjects, thus providing basic knowledge of photographic skill as the basic for further development.
